About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and AI risk.

Through collaboration with academia and industry, IDR prepares professionals to address emerging technological risks and support organisations navigating complex digital ecosystems.

Our Core Model

Academy

Training programs and intensive bootcamps for students and professionals developing expertise in digital risk, cybersecurity, and AI governance.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing intellectual property, advanced risk frameworks and new models for AI governance and emerging technology oversight.

Advisory Services

Supporting organisations implementing digital risk frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Our Community
